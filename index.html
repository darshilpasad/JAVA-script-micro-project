What We Monitor

Water Quality & pH

Use Water Quality Index and pH to understand overall conditions and acidity or alkalinity.

Aquatic Health

Track dissolved oxygen, salinity, turbidity, and bacterial levels that affect marine life.

Pollution Detection

Review oil spills, plastic waste, microplastics, nutrient runoff, and heavy metals.

Why Water Quality Matters

Protect Coastal Communities

Clear, timely water-quality information helps identify potential health risks and supports safer coastal recreation and fisheries.

Protect Marine Ecosystems

Reducing plastic, nutrient, oil, and heavy-metal pollution helps protect coral reefs, fish, turtles, and other marine species.

Sustainable Development Goals

SDG 6 – Clean Water and Sanitation

Monitoring water quality supports safer water resources and helps reveal contamination.

SDG 14 – Life Below Water

Water-quality monitoring supports healthy oceans and reduces marine pollution.
